Trappey in Proceedings of the 2018 International Conference on Industrial Enterprise and System Engineering (IcoIESE 2018). We implemented LDA model for topic modeling which identified relationships between terms and topics explaining underlying IP dynamics.
  • Media Publications
    • "India: A Powell-ian deconstruction of India's CPI", with Sajjid Z Chinoy & Toshi Jain in Bloomberg, Oct 2021, JP Morgan. This was a research note assessing whether inflation was being driven by a few outliers or was more broad-based. We created three different inflation estimates for India's CPI basket (~300 time series)- 'diffusion index', 'trimmed mean index' and 'disproportionality index'.
  • JP Morgan Publications
    • "US: Big data batting 1-for-1", with Jesse Edgerton & Dan Weitzenfeld, Jun 2020, JP Morgan. This was a research note explaining how we identified 20+ high-frequency alternative data series during the pandemic to assess its real-time impact. We designed and performed daily data aggregation of alt-data to complement traditional forecasting of economic data.
